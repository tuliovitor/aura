# Project Brief: Studio Aura Digital Presence

## 1. Project Overview
**Client:** Marina Vasconcelos, Founder & Lead Architect
**Brand:** Studio Aura
**Industry:** High-end Interior Design & Architecture
**Objective:** Create a sophisticated, immersive landing page that communicates "discreet luxury" and attracts high-net-worth clients in São Paulo.

## 2. Brand Identity & Visual Strategy
*   **Design Philosophy:** "Luxury doesn't scream; it reveals itself in the silence of details."
*   **Visual Direction:** Boutique hotel meets art gallery. Minimalist, elegant, and atmospheric.
*   **Color Palette (Aura System):**
    *   Primary: Deep Charcoal / Carbon (#131313)
    *   Accent: Champagne Gold / Metallic accents
*   **Typography:** *Bodoni Moda* (Serif) for headings to provide an editorial, high-fashion feel.
*   **Key Imagery:** Dark, textured interiors, noble materials (wood, stone, metal), and professional portraiture.

## 3. Core Experience Requirements
1.  **Immersive Hero:** High-impact background video (`interior-dark-video.mp4`) to establish immediate mood.
2.  **Narrative Process:** A 3-step "Essence in Layers" section using video (`video-camadas.mp4`) to show technical depth.
3.  **Curated Portfolio:** Display of 6-7 premium projects with varying aspect ratios to create visual rhythm.
4.  **Founder's Story:** Dedicated section for Marina Vasconcelos to humanize the brand and build trust.
5.  **Social Proof:** High-fidelity testimonials from satisfied homeowners and collectors.
6.  **Conversion Path:** Minimalist inquiry form and direct WhatsApp integration for private consultations.

## 4. Technical Specifications
*   **Device Target:** Desktop (Primary focus for high-end professional impact).
*   **Interaction Design:** Smooth transitions, backdrop blurs (glassmorphism), and subtle hover effects to reinforce the "luxury experience."
*   **Platform:** High-performance HTML/CSS with responsive adaptations.

## 5. Content Assets
*   **Hero Video:** `interior-dark-video.mp4`
*   **Process Video:** `video-camadas.mp4`
*   **Images:** Professional interiors and portraits provided in the DataStore.
